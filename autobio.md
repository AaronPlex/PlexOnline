**Autobiography**

I had never expected to make it to the list of selected ones but I was among the top 10 best candidates. 
My failures... Was my professor putting his faith on me? I remind myself about this as a tipping point of my life. Had I failed to pass that class, perhaps I could never have continued in higher education (financially and emotionally). High school taught me that success can come from exceptional people believing on me (even if you never know them on a daily basis).

My colleagues used to call me "alumbrado" ("enlightened"). I was not.
